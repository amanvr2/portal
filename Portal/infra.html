INFRASTRUCTURE



* Workshops :-


The workshops are headed by The Workshop InchargeS Mr. JS LAMBA AND Mr. ADESH TYAGI.
Institute have the following workshops :->

  1. CAPRENTRY WORKSHOP
  2. FITTING WORKSHOP
  3. MACHINE WORKSHOP
  4. SHEETMETAL WORKSHOP
  5. WEILDING SHOP

CARPENTRY WORKSHOP

The workshop has a capacity of accommodating 15-16 students at a time. Students are made familiar with the known tools and practices of the Carpentry Workshop. During their course of study, different types of jobs and joints are made by students.


FITTING WORKSHOP

The workshop consists of 4 working benches and 16 bench vices besides machines like Grinder and Drilling Machine. Students are given vigorous training of working with common tools like hacksaw and files. Different types of joints and jobs are made by students.


MACHINE WORKSHOP

The Machine Shop consists of very expensive and sophisticated machines like Lethe Machine, Grinding Machine, Shaper, Universal Milling Machine, Slotter Machine and Radial Drilling Machine etc.


SHEET METAL WORKSHOP

The Machine Shop consists of very expensive and sophisticated machines like Lethe Machine, Grinding Machine, Shaper, Universal Milling Machine, Slotter Machine and Radial Drilling Machine etc.


WEILDING WORKSHOP

The wielding workshop consists of an Air Cool Transformer and an Oil Based Transformer. Students are taught the basic principles of safe and effective wielding techniques.
